We believe in transparency. This page explains clearly what data we collect, why we collect it, and what rights you have.

1. Who We Are

YouthTICK is a youth initiative based in Yalova, Türkiye, working in the fields of civic participation, intercultural dialogue and Erasmus+ youth cooperation. We are in our foundation phase, established in 2026.

For the purpose of this policy, YouthTICK acts as the data controller for information collected through this website.

Contact: info@youthtick.org
Address: Yalova, Türkiye

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only what we need. Here is a clear breakdown:

  • Contact form submissions — Your name, email address and message when you write to us via our contact or partnership forms.
  • Newsletter subscriptions — Your email address if you choose to subscribe to our digest.
  • Volunteer applications — Your name, email address, area of interest and any information you voluntarily provide in your application.
  • Analytics data — If you accept analytics cookies, we collect anonymised information about how you navigate the site (pages visited, time spent, referral source). This is processed by Google Analytics 4. We do not collect personally identifiable analytics data.

We do not collect payment information, sell data to third parties, or use tracking for advertising purposes.

3. Why We Collect It (Legal Basis)

  • Contact & enquiry forms — Legitimate interest: to respond to your enquiry.
  • Newsletter — Consent: you explicitly signed up and can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Volunteer applications — Legitimate interest: to process your application and respond.
  • Analytics — Consent: only activated if you accept analytics cookies via our cookie banner.

4. Cookies

We use two categories of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — Required for the website to function (e.g. remembering your language preference and cookie consent choice). These are always active.
  • Analytics cookies — Set by Google Analytics 4 to help us understand how visitors find and use the site. These are only activated if you click "Accept All" in our cookie banner.

You can change your cookie preference at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or by contacting us.

5. How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Contact & partner enquiries: up to 24 months, unless you request deletion earlier.
  • Newsletter subscriptions: until you unsubscribe.
  • Volunteer applications: up to 12 months after the relevant recruitment period.
  • Analytics data: in line with Google Analytics data retention settings (default: 14 months).

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area or a country with equivalent data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — Ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure — Request that we delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restriction — Ask us to limit how we process your data.
  • Right to data portability — Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object — Object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to withdraw consent — Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time without affecting prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@youthtick.org. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Security

We may update this policy as our operations develop. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
