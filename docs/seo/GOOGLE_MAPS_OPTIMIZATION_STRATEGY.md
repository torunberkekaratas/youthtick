## ⚠️ COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

### 1. **Keyword Stuffing**
❌ Bad: "Yalova Erasmus Yalova gençlik Yalova başvuru..."  
✅ Good: Natural language with keywords sprinkled

### 2. **Fake Reviews**
❌ Bad: Asking family/staff to review  
✅ Good: Genuine customers only

### 3. **Inconsistent NAP**
❌ Bad: Different address on website vs GBP  
✅ Good: Exact same NAP everywhere

### 4. **Ignoring Negative Reviews**
