Who We Are

A new kind of youth organization.

YouthTICK operates at the intersection of international mobility, civic participation and entrepreneurial thinking — creating spaces where young people don't just participate, they lead.

We focus on youth civic participation, intercultural dialogue, entrepreneurship and sustainability — areas where we believe young people from Türkiye can play a meaningful and lasting role in European youth cooperation.

Our Story
Our Vision

Europe's next decade will be defined by young people who understand technology, embrace diversity, and act with conviction.

We believe in building institutions that outlast moments — creating lasting change through international collaboration, civic engagement and cultural intelligence.
