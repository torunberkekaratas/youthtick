What We Run

Types of events we are building

Each format serves a different purpose — from information to connection to action. Here is what you can expect from YouthTICK events.

Erasmus+ Info Sessions
Practical online sessions where we walk through how Erasmus+ works, what Turkish youth organisations are eligible for, how to find partners, and what a first application looks like. No jargon. Honest information.
Online · 90 minutes · Free
Civic Engagement Workshops
In-person workshops in Yalova focusing on themes like youth participation, local governance, environmental action or digital citizenship. Designed for 12–25 participants. Interactive, dialogue-based format.
Yalova · Half-day · Free
Intercultural Dialogue Sessions
Structured conversations between young people from different countries and cultural backgrounds. Small groups, facilitated discussions, real exchange — not performative "diversity" but honest dialogue about shared challenges.
Online + In-person · 2 hours · Free
Partner Network Calls
Regular online calls for organisations interested in Erasmus+ cooperation with YouthTICK. A space to introduce your work, explore shared interests and begin conversations that may lead to real partnerships.
Online · Monthly · For organisations
Sustainability Action Days
Community action events in Yalova focused on environmental themes — urban greening, sustainability workshops, local ecological projects. Open to all young people in the area, no experience required.
Yalova · Full-day · Free
Youth Entrepreneurship Talks
Conversations with young people who are building social enterprises, civic initiatives or community projects. Real stories from real founders — honest about what worked and what didn't. No motivational speakers.
Online · 2 hours · Free
