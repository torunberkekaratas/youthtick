Germany–Turkey Axis

Two cultures. One shared vision.

The Germany–Turkey youth corridor is at the heart of YouthTICK\'s work. With over 3 million people of Turkish origin living in Germany, this relationship holds enormous potential for cultural diplomacy and mutual understanding.

We are exploring what genuine bilateral dialogue might look like — not as a finished program, but as an honest conversation between young people from both countries.
