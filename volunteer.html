YouthTICK is a fully volunteer-based initiative. Every role here is unpaid — we are a community of people who join because they want to grow, learn and build connections, not because they are collecting a salary.

🌱 Volunteer-only initiative. No paid positions exist or will be created. We are here to develop skills, build networks and learn together.
