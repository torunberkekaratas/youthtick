FAQ

Common questions.

How quickly do you respond to enquiries?
We aim to respond to all messages within 48 working hours. For urgent matters, use the subject "Urgent" in your message.
Can I apply if I\'m outside Europe?
Most of our Erasmus+ programs are limited to EU/Erasmus+ programme countries, but we have associate partnerships globally. Reach out to discuss your situation.
Do you offer paid positions?
No, YouthTICK is an independent initiative and all roles are strictly volunteer-based. We focus on mutual learning, self-improvement, and networking.
How do I propose a joint Erasmus+ project?
Send us a partnership enquiry with a brief description of your organisation and project idea. Our team will review and respond within 5 working days.
