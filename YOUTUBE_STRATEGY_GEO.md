## 🎬 PRODUCTION TIPS

### Equipment (Budget-Friendly)
- **Camera:** iPhone 12+ or any modern smartphone (4K capable)
- **Microphone:** Rode VideoMic Me ($59) or similar lavalier mic
- **Lighting:** Natural window light + $30 LED panel
- **Editing:** CapCut (free), DaVinci Resolve (free), or iMovie

### Visual Style
- Clean, professional but authentic
- B-roll of Yalova, participants, activities
- Use graphics/text overlays for key points
- Subtitles in BOTH English and Turkish (accessibility + AI parsing)

### Thumbnail Strategy
- High contrast text
- Faces (when possible) — increases CTR
- YouthTICK branding (logo in corner)
- Consistent color scheme (blue/white from brand)

## 📈 GROWTH STRATEGY

### Upload Frequency
**Month 1-2:** 1 video/week (build foundation)
**Month 3-6:** 2 videos/week (gain momentum)
**Month 6+:** 3 videos/week (establish authority)

### Content Mix (60/30/10 Rule)
- **60%** Educational/How-to (Erasmus+ guides, application tips)
- **30%** Storytelling (participant stories, day-in-the-life)
- **10%** Promotional (program announcements, calls for participants)

### Promotion Strategy
1. Share on Instagram Reels (cut 60s highlights)
2. Embed on website (especially FAQ and opportunities pages)
3. Share in relevant Facebook groups (Erasmus+ Turkey groups)
4. Email newsletter to subscribers
5. Share with partner organizations
